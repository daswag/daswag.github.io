What is daSWAG ?

daSWAG is a development platform to generate, develop and deploy full Serverless Web applications.

Use cases

How can I use daSWAG in my organisation?

Creating a new applications

Building new projects to address new business needs is a core part of any entreprise. With daSWAG you can generate your production ready projet in seconds and deploy it to your favorite cloud providers.

Building an architecture adapted to your futur needs

Building an infrastructure adapted to your needs and scalable over time is a daily challenge. The daSWAG generator uses only Serverless technologies that guarantee a perfect and automatic matching of your infrastructure to your traffic pattern.

Deploying an highly available and cost Optimized application

Serverless technologies are services managed by Cloud providers. They thus guarantee you a high availability and to pay only for what you consume (pay as you go). The daSWAG project is built around these technologies and will provide you with all these advantages natively.
